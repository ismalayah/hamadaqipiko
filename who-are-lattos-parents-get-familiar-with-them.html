Alyssa Michelle Stephens, better known by her stage names Latto and Big Latto (formerly known as Mulatto), was born on December 22, 1998, in the United States. She made her first appearance on the reality competition show The Rap Game hosted by American rapper Jermaine Dupri in 2016 as Miss Mulatto.

Latto’s Career

Despite winning the first season of the show, she declined the record deal that was offered as a prize. Latto joined RCA Records after releasing her 2019 single “Bitch from da Souf.” In August 2020, the song debuted on the Billboard Hot 100, reaching a high of 95.
