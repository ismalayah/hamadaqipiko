Tiphani Montgomery Called Beyonc A Witch In Her Tweet Sparking A Social Debate

Publish date: 2024-05-06

Tiphani Montgomery, the pastor who founded the Covered by God organization, has sparked outrage on social media after calling Beyoncé a “witch” in a now-viral video. Montgomery can be heard in the video berating Christians who are fans of the singer. The lecture, which has gone viral on social media, includes a tape of the pastor “rebuking” individuals who attend the singer’s performance.

Tiphani Montgomery uploaded a video to TikTok, which has gotten over 20,000 views. She wrote in the caption:

“Baalyonce doesn’t even disguise her devotion to her several gods. The devil granted her INFLUENCE over MILLIONS, and she successfully exploits her platform for the kingdom of darkness.”

Was Tiphani Montgomery a Beyoncé fan? Netizens call her a “hypocrite”

Tiphani may be heard saying in the TikTok video:

“I admonish anybody attending Beyoncé’s performance in the name of Jesus. You have no right to call yourself a Christian. I don’t care who your pastor is who is okay with it, who your pastor thinks it’s fine, and who your pastor sings along to the tunes because they want some clout.”

Tiphani not only branded Beyoncé a “witch,” but she also called out the singer’s followers, saying:

“It’s not uncommon for a witch to have a coven. The covens of witches are often made up of three to seven members. When it reaches thousands, it is referred to as a hive. Are you a member of that woman Beyhive? And you identify as a Christian? May the Lord’s dealings fall upon you.”

Others started looking into Tiphani Montgomery’s social media profile after the video went viral, and discovered tweets from 2010, 2011, and 2014 in which she declared that she liked Beyoncé’s music and claimed to have attended several of her concerts.

Several social media users labeled her a “hypocrite” since she was now bashing Beyoncé fans. Montgomery, on the other hand, addressed her earlier remarks, claiming that she was the greatest Beyoncé and Jay Z fan until August 2015.

What you need to know about Tiphani Montgomery in the middle of the Beyoncé saga

Many were anxious to learn more about Tiphani Montgomery after she stepped onstage and shouted out Beyoncé. Tiphani, who was born on July 4, is a preacher and Gospel music performer. She often produces movies and puts them on TikTok, where she shares her lessons with her 21,000 fans.

Her website states that she assists those who are stuck in their businesses or in their lives. According to her website, she also assists people in starting businesses without “selling your soul for money.” According to her website, she offers a variety of training classes, publications, live events, and conferences to help individuals remain connected with God.
