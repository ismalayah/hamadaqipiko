Miz And Mrs. Renewed For Season 2

Publish date: 2024-08-19

During this week’s episode of SmackDown Live, The Miz and Maryse announced that USA Network has picked up their show, Miz And Mrs., for a second season.

https://twitter.com/mikethemiz/status/1029526087586512896

The show premiered on July 24 and drew in around 1.47 million viewers for the first episode. The fourth episode of the show is going to air after SmackDown Live, with two more to go for the first season.
