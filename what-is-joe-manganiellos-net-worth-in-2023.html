What is Joe Manganiellos net worth in 2023?

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Joe Manganiello is an American actor and filmmaker who is best known for his roles in Spider-Man, Magic Mike, and True Blood. He’s been active in Hollywood for over 20 years, earning him a net worth of $40 million, according to CelebrityNetWorth.com

More recently, Manganiello appeared in the 2023 film Magic Mike’s Last Dance. Variety reported in 2022 that he would be directing a Dungeons & Dragons documentary as well.

He also announced an upcoming project called Nonnas, which will be a comedy featuring Susan Sarandon and Vince Vaughn. His new comedy-thriller, The Kill Room with Samuel L. Jackson, Maya Hawke, and Uma Thurman, is expected to hit theaters this Fall.

Manganiello is one of many actors who is participating in the SAG Writers’ and Actors’ strike, he shared a post on his Instagram communicating his support.

In current news, Manganiello and his wife Sofia Vergara shared news of their divorce with Page Six on July 17. The couple has been married for 7 years after meeting at the 2014 White House Correspondents’ Dinnee. They were introduced by their mutual friend, Jesse Tyler Ferguson.

The pair has been sparking divorce rumors for a while, with Vargara on a birthday trip, and Manganiello (and a wedding ring) nowhere to be found. Their joint statement asked fans for, “respect of our privacy at this time as [they] navigate this new phase of [their] lives.”
