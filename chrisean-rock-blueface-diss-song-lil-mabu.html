Chrisean Rock Clowns Blueface On Diss Song With 'New Bae' Lil Mabu

Publish date: 2024-09-20

Chrisean Rock has moved on from Blueface, and now he’s feeling her wrath on a diss record alongside her apparent “new bae,” Lil Mabu.

Released on Monday (October 16), “MR. TAKE YA B*TCH” finds the Baltimore native and her boo bragging about their new relationship and rubbing it in her child’s father’s face.
