Who Was Tom T Hall Wife Dixie Hall?

Tom T Hall was married to his wife Iris Dixie Hall for 48 years and they were married until her death in January of 2015.

Tom and Dixie, as professional musicians met each other at not a very young age since they were both nearly 40 when they got married.

It is reported that they first met at a musical event that happened in 1965. Then, they probably started seeing each other and eventually got married a few years later.

Regarding Dixie Hall as an individual, she is a native of Great Britain and she moved to the United States in 1961.

Like her husband, Dixie is also a renowned musician who has been a part of numerous popular musical events in American history.

Indeed, she wrote more than 500 commercially recorded bluegrass songs. With this record, she became the female singer with the most written songs.

Apart from her professional life, she gave birth to the singer Dean Hall with her husband Tom T Hall.
