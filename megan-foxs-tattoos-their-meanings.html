Megan Foxs 7 Tattoos &amp; Their Meanings

Publish date: 2024-05-06

Megan Fox has won the hearts of millions with her stunning beauty and acting abilities, but it’s not only her beauty that has gotten to us. Megan Fox has a fantastic array of body arts, adding to her allure. All her body tats aren’t just for show; each has a story behind it. Megan Fox’s tats celebrate who she is and are mainly devoted to the important things in her life, according to her. Everything you need to know about Megan Fox’s tattoos and their significance is right here.

1. ‘Yin Yang’ Tattoo

Tattoo: Her left wrist has the tattoo ‘Yin Yang.’

Meaning: The tattoo depicts two overlapping waves in the shape of the Chinese character Yin Yang. The Chinese spiritual symbol Yin Yang represents the balance of good and evil. According to media sources, Megan said that she is unhappy with her tattoo and plans to have it laser removed soon, yet she is still spotted flaunting her ‘Yin Yang’ tattoo on different occasions to this day.

2. ‘Poem’ Tattoo

Tattoo: ‘Poem’ tattooed on the left side of her ribcage.

Meaning: A lovely poem penned by Megan herself is inked in the Victorian typeface on the left side of her rib cage. According to the poem, “there once was a young girl who never experienced love until a guy shattered her HEART.” There were allegations that she had this tattoo done by Mickey Rourke, with whom she allegedly had a romance while shooting together on the film Passion Play,’ which was released on May 6, 2011.

3. ‘Marilyn Monroe’ Tattoo

Tattoo: On her right forearm, she has a tattoo of Marilyn Monroe.

Meaning: Megan Fox’s Marilyn Monroe tat is one of her cutest tattoos. Megan has been a fan of Marilyn Monroe since she was a youngster, and she claims to have wept every time she heard Marilyn’s voice as a child. She received this when she was 18 years old, but in 2011, she chose to have the picture erased through laser therapy in order to prevent bad energy in her life. In a 2012 interview, she revealed the complex laser therapy she is undergoing to remove this tattoo.

4. ‘Chinese Symbol for Strength’ Tattoo

Tattoo: On the nape of her neck, she has a tattoo of the Chinese symbol for strength.

Meaning: Megan has a tiny Chinese sign (力) tattooed on the back of her neck that means “strong.”

5. ‘Shakespeare’s Quote’ Tattoo

Tattoo: On the right side of her back, she has a tattoo of Shakespeare’s quote.

Meaning: The phrase “We shall all laugh at Gilded Butterflies” from Shakespeare’s “King Lear” is inked in Victorian type on Megan’s right shoulder blade. Though Megan has somewhat changed it to reflect her true nature. Megan is often spotted showing off her tattoos on different occasions.

6. ‘Crescent Moon and Star’ Tattoo

Tattoo: Near her right ankle, she has a tattoo of a crescent moon and a star.

Meaning: Megan had a tattoo of a yellow and orange crescent moon that overlaps the blue and orange star tattooed near her ankle. Megan has just one colorful tattoo on her body. The crescent moon with a star symbolizes divine powers, wisdom, knowledge, blessings, optimism, and protection.

7. ‘Quote inspired by Nietzsche’ Tattoo

Tattoo: On the right side of her chest, she has a tattoo of a quote inspired by Nietzsche.

Meaning: Megan seems to be influenced by Nietzsche, a German philosopher, poet, and composer. “And those who were observed dancing were believed to be crazy by those who could not hear the music,” Nietzsche said. Mickey Rourke, the actor, was honored with the tattoo. Megan had a romance with the actor when they were both working on the film Passion Play (2010).
